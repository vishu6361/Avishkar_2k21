RasayansChem-E-Brain

How handy do you think your knowledge of Chemical Engineering is? Chem-E-Brain is here for inspection

About

Pump plays an important role in supplying energy to transport or compress fluids. They are the most significant accessory used in the chemical industry. There are multiple types of pumps namely centrifugal pump, diaphragm pump, piston pump and so on. Centrifugal pumps are the primary group types in the class of pumps known as “kinetic” pumps. The main parts of the centrifugal pump are the impeller which is used to increase kinetic energy of the flow and the shaft (motor) onto which impeller is mounted. Along with pumps, components like valves are used for flow regulation. CHEM-E-BRAIN is an event that provides you a challenging model making problem where you will deal with fluid. Come up with an innovative idea so that you can design an efficient centrifugal pump and a valve under specified constraints.

Participation Criteria

Team size : 2-3
Years participating:
B. Tech - II year
B. Tech - III year
